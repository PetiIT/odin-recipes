Long sauce

Description

Carrot stew is easy to make. Delicious. It is worth making it from fresh baby carrots.

Good preparation and bon appetit.

Ingredients

  • 15 dkg carrots
  • 2 dl milk
  • 2 dl of water
  • 3 dkg fine flour
  • 1 pinch of sugar
  • salt to taste
  • 2 tablespoons of sunflower oil

Steps

  1. Clean and grate the carrot.
  2. Add a tablespoon of water and simmer in the oil until soft.
  3. Remove from the heat, sprinkle the flour over it, mix well, then mix with the milk and water until there are no lumps.
  4. We put it back on the fire and bring it to a boil.
  5. Depending on what it is being prepared for, add sugar and salt.
